# Nightingale Connect - User Guide

## Welcome to Nightingale Connect!

Nightingale Connect is a secure, professional communication platform designed specifically for South African nurse practitioners. This guide will help you understand and navigate the platform.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. **Account Registration**
- Visit the platform and click "Sign In" 
- Use your Replit account to register
- Your account will be automatically approved for immediate access
- You'll be assigned the "nurse" role by default

### 2. **First Login**
- After successful registration, you'll see the main chat interface
- If there are platform announcements, they'll appear in a red banner
- Click anywhere outside the announcement to dismiss it

---

## Main Features Overview

### 🗨️ **Real-Time Chat**
- **Multiple Channels**: Join different discussion channels (General Discussion, Emergency Care, etc.)
- **Message Replies**: Click the reply button on any message to respond directly
- **File Attachments**: Upload documents, images, and other files up to 10MB
- **Color-Coded Messages**: 
  - Regular messages: Standard blue/gray theme
  - Reply messages: Purple theme for better visual distinction
  - Messages with attachments: Green theme

### 🤖 **AI-Powered Assistance**
- AI automatically analyzes relevant medical discussions
- Provides intelligent suggestions based on FAQs and educational content
- AI suggestions appear below messages with helpful information
- Click on FAQ or education links in suggestions to learn more

### 📚 **Knowledge Resources**

#### **FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)**
- Access through the "❓ FAQ" button in chat
- Search by category or keywords
- Categories include: Billing, Patient Care, Procedures, Regulations, etc.
- AI suggestions often link directly to relevant FAQ answers

#### **Educational Resources**
- Access through the "📖 Education" button in chat
- Browse blog posts, guidelines, procedures, and policies
- Filter by category or search by keywords
- Featured content appears at the top
- Download attachments and documents

### 🗺️ **Find Practitioners**
- Access through the map icon in the header
- Connect with other nurse practitioners in your area
- View practitioners by location across South Africa
- Build your professional network regionally

---

## How to Use the Chat Interface

### **Sending Messages**
1. Type your message in the text box at the bottom
2. Attach files using the paperclip icon (optional)
3. Click "Send" or press Enter

### **Replying to Messages**
1. Click the "Reply" button on any message
2. You'll see a preview of the original message
3. Type your response and send
4. Your reply will appear in purple with a link to the original

### **Viewing AI Suggestions**
1. AI suggestions appear automatically below relevant messages
2. Click "View FAQ" to see related frequently asked questions
3. Click "View Education" to access related educational content
4. These suggestions help you find verified information quickly

## Understanding User Roles

### **Nurse (Default Role)**
- Join chat discussions
- Access FAQs and educational content
- Upload and share files
- Reply to messages
- View practitioner directory

### **Senior Nurse**
- All nurse privileges
- Additional moderation capabilities
- Help validate community content

### **Admin**
- Full platform management access
- Content creation and editing
- User management
- Platform announcements

---

## Platform Etiquette & Guidelines

### **Professional Communication**
- Maintain professional language and tone
- Respect patient privacy - never share identifiable patient information
- Be supportive and collaborative with colleagues
- Stay on-topic in relevant channels

### **Content Sharing**
- Share educational resources that benefit the community
- Verify information before sharing medical advice
- Use appropriate channels for different types of discussions
- Credit sources when sharing external content

### **Patient Privacy Protection**
- **Never include patient names, ID numbers, or identifying details**
- Use general terms when discussing cases
- The AI system helps detect potential privacy issues
- When in doubt, keep discussions general and educational

## Notifications & Announcements

### **Platform Announcements**
- Important platform updates appear in red banners
- Announcements show once per day to each user
- Click outside the banner to dismiss
- Critical information will be highlighted

### **Activity Notifications**
- New messages in channels you've joined
- Replies to your messages
- AI suggestions relevant to your discussions
- Platform updates and maintenance notices
